About Ben

Hey! My name is Ben Aronson and this is my website. I work as a software engineer currently based in Boston, MA.


My approach to life could be closer described as a breadth-first search rather than depth-first (lol). I generally gravitate towards projects, people, and passions that are creative, curious, and mission-driven.


My education in software engineering, experience in consulting, and knack for creativity, along with a strong sense of empathy, enables me to forge cross-disciplinary connections between people and ideas.


I'm still searching for the type of role where I can provide the most impact, but I seem to do well leading small teams on projects with clear goals amongst a larger organization with a strong sense of purpose and favorable view on innovation.

About Concourse

Concourse Codes is an attempt to create a digital confluence of all the ideas, projects, endeavors, and experiences I have to share with the world.


Fellow ADHD-ers will relate to the natural impulse towards new and shiny things. Instead of viewing our many initatives as failed endeavors, I am on a mission to reframe them as a singular, holistic catalog of our interests, passions, and skills.
Here, you can read some of my thoughts, experiment with some of my projects, and, if you're observant, explore some of my creative endeavors.
