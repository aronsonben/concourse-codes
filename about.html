About Concourse

Concourse Codes is an attempt to create a digital confluence of all the ideas, projects, endeavors, and experiences I have to share with the world.


Fellow ADHD-ers will relate to the natural impulse towards new and shiny things. While this can be a challenge at times, I also believe that this is a beautiful feature of this condition. It allows us to experience a broad swath of the human experience, gain deep perspective, and embrace childlike joy in discovery and imagination.


Thus, I hope you find something interesting here.
